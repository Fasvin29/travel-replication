Rules

Before entering the past, there are things one cannot do. Upon going back to the present, they may not take anything from the past, they cannot kill anything unauthorized by the guides, they cannot stray from the path, and they cannot speak of this trip in the present.

Safety

It’s advised participants do not bring anything back from the past or the course of the future will change. Any damage done to the participant isn’t under the responsibilities of Chronoshift. Under the Guide’s instruction if followed properly, participants will remain safe.

Risks

Participants are not to bring anything or kill anything unauthorized from the past. This will change the course of time and could possibly endanger the stability of what they know to be the present and the future. The participant may also get hurt or lose their life, Chronoshift is not held accountable.

Penalties

Upon returning, if any rules are broken by any participant, legal action will be taken along with a penalty fee of ten thousand dollars. Any damage done to anything else will be later determined.

For this trip, you will need to bring yourself, food, and safety/exploration/hunting gear depending on what you plan to do there.
